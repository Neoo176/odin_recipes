Perfection Salad !

One of the best American recipe

Who doesn't love a good salad in gelatine guys ? I sure do

This meal takes needs a lot of time to cook, make sure you have at least a full ay ahead of you

Ingredients

  • 2 envelopes unflavored gelatine
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 can (12 ml) apple juice
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar
  • 1 cup shredded carrot
  • 1 cup sliced celery
  • 1 cup finely shredded cabbage
  • 1/2 cup chopped green pepper
  • 1 can (4 oz) chopped pimiento

Steps:

  1. In small saucepan, combine gelatine, sugar, and salt; mix well.
  2. Add 1 cup water. Heat over low heat, stirring constantly, until sugar and gelatine are dissolved. Remove from heat.
  3. Stir in apple juicy, cabbage, green pepper, and pimiento; stir until well combined.
  4. Turn into decorative, 1 1/2-quart mold. Refrigerate 4 hours,or until firm.
  5. To unmold: Run small spatula around edge of mold; invert onto serving plate. Place hot dishcloth over mold; shake gently to release. Repeat, if necessary. Lift off mold. refrigerate until ready to serve.
  6. Enjoy your healthy jelly salad
make 8 servings
