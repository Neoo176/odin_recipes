Pilk

A rare picture of the beverage of the gods

In this recipe we shall attempt to recreate this drink, while it may look fairly simple, the steps involved needs precise execution and the finest cuisine skills

Whatever happens, do not replace Pepsi with Coca Cola

Ingredients:

  • Pepsi: 330ml
  • Milk: 200ml
  • Chocolate powder: one big spoon

Steps

  1. Grab a big glass, a can of pepsi and the milk
  2. Pour the milk first into the glass, very carefully
  3. Grab the chocolate powder in your closet or place of choice and fill a spoon with it

    4. This next step need to be done as fast as possible, the quality of the drink will depend on it

  4. Pour the pepsi down the bottle and immediately empty the chocolate powder spoon in the glass. Do not stir
  5. Enjoy your pilk!!!!
